I wanted to post a little thing on Legion rank Shorthand. To help make some things make sense. I came up with a simpler way of describing an Astartes rank. We will be Using Chapter Master Ezekyle Abbadon for this example.

Master Abbadon XVI-I-II-0-0-Cohort-Alpha
1. Rank or position so stuff like Battle-Brother or Techmarine.

2. the Ascension Name. When an Astartes is fully grown they pick a new last name to signify their place as an Angel of Death. This is often done because Battle-Brothers cannot remember their old family name. Abaddon kept his Chthonian Gang-name due to remembering it and wanting to keep a connection to his first tribe.

3. the Legion Number.

4. The Formation they belong to. All Astartes even ones not operating in a Formation structure are assigned to one. Allowing them to take up that role if needed.

5. The Chapter they belong too if this is not applicable a Zero is marked instead

6. The company they belong to, if this is not applicable a Zero is marked instead

7. The Squad they belong to, if this is not applicable a Zero is marked instead.

8./9. The Cohort they are part of. Listed last because it is the one most likely to shift.

Yes, it's complicated and Neurotic. What else do you expect from the Imperium of Man?
