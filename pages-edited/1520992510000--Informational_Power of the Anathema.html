So something I wanna explain before we get to the next part of the story is how exactly the Emperor effects the Warp. The Emperor of Mankind is an Anathema and his very existence and nature calms the Warp. He is the Lord of the Sane, of the Real and he acts as focal point for mortals and the Warp to focus and ground themselves. The Warp is a reflection of the Material and when sanity prevails the Warp starts to change. This is what I meant when I referred to the Emperor "Poisoning the Ocean" in "Smoke on the Water, Fire in the Warp" An Anathema is a being that taps into the desire for logic and order in all sentient beings just as the Chaos gods tap into unrestrained emotions and concepts. (this will be explored more in detail later in the story, along with what exactly IS an Anathema and where they come from) The Anathemas interaction with the Warp can be classified in a few tiers.

  • Icon- The Anathema is a symbol and object of devotion. The Material Realm is protected from Chaos passively and mythotypes related to the Anathema have powers against Chaos. (Basically the Golden Throne Emperor)
  • Paragon- The Anathema brings order where ever it goes and actively battles the forces of Chaos and provides a counterbalance to the Warp and starts to force some elements of restraint and sanity into the Warp (Great Crusade Era Emperor)
  • Master of Sanity- The Immaterial starts to bend to the Anathemas will and Chaos state as Masters of the Immaterium is challenged and their influence is muted. (The Astronomicon was the start of this)
  • Lord of Order- The Chaos Gods Face an equal of incredible power who actively threatens there existence and parts of the Realms of Chaos become Realms of Order. (The Farthest an Anathema ever has gotten and the God-Emperor may be turning into a mutilated version of this.)
  • Ascendant- The Anathema's influence becomes the dominant power in the Warp, Chaos starts to starve as mortal beings start to become like the Anathema and Ascended past the influence and taint of Chaos. (The Emperor and his predecessor's goal where the Chaos Gods would fade into myths and the Material is Master of its reflection not a slave to it.
Yet as the Anathema climbs these tiers Chaos actually gets more dangerous until they reach the Ascendant level. As the Warp becomes more ordered the Chaos Gods become increasingly desperate like a feral animal backed into a corner. The order and logic pressed into the warp also start to allow the Chaos Gods to start functioning at a higher level. They become more organized and capable of higher reasoning. This can be seen during the Heresy when Chaos defied its own nature to unite and organize against the Emperor. Now as the Anathema grows and power things are going to get a lot worse before they get better. The Chaos Gods and their minions will become capable of long-term planning and organization at a truly horrifying level.
