Next part of Chapter 13 is on its way and I'm doing some research into the Hexagrammaton organization pattern of early Astartes. I am thinking of organizing the Legions a bit differently than canon. Also, something that might rankle some people but I feel is necessary is I am going to be increasing the sizes of the Legions significantly.

The numbers given in the Horus Heresy novels just don't add up. Sure Codex Astartes 1000 man strike force organization makes sense but fighting galactic campaigns with only a 100,000 or so frontline troops is utterly ridiculous. If the lore depicted them as strike forces sure it could make sense but you can't wage galactic warfare against enemies equivalent in might (Orks, Rangdans, other post-human groups) with those numbers. The Legions are going to be at least a few million in number simply because they have to be for any of this to make sense. Sure an Astartes is worth an entire regiment of humans and can crush entire human armies with a few hundred troops, well what about trillions of Orks under the banner of a Pseudo-Beast?

Chalk it up to BL not getting the scale of the universe they designed to be utterly colossal, but the Legio Astartes are gonna have to be a lot bigger for any of this to work.
