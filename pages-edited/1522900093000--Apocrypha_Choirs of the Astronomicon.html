(This is only semi-Canon and not to be taken seriously.)

Eldrad Ulthran was a rising star among the Seer Community of Ulthwé, he had saved his Craftworld and many others numerous times thanks to his incredible gift of foresight. He was commonly believed to be the greatest Psyker of his kind since the Fall. An event he had barely lived through as a youth and that still was a raw wound upon the psyche of his people. Even now his home was stuck on the edge of the slowly expanding Abomination that had once been the Hallowed Crone Worlds.

Eldrad worked night and day scrying the threads of fate for the best possible future of his people. He saw futures where a Giant made of Light broke into the Labyrinth Dimension and brought doom to his people, where Old Foes of there forebearers slithered back from there banishment to feed, and truly horrific ones where the galaxy burned in the fires of demi-gods manipulated ambition. Yet the events that currently were unfolding were unknowns to him and never hinted at even during his deepest delving into the Sea of Souls. A Wave of Psychic Power had blanketed the Galaxy and burned away at both the former Crone Worlds and them.

Things made of psychic fire and primitive souls screamed through the void and crashed into the Craftworld. Eldrad watched in horror as winged things made of soul-flames roared through the void. They hardly seemed to notice the Craftworld and washed over it like a gout of warp-born solar wind. The Seer could only watch in shock as there Ancestors Folly shrunk before his very eyes as the flames scorched away the corruption. After the initial shock the Craftworld started to move, they were no longer locked in the Follies gravity well and were sailing free of it. The Eldar were transfixed as they felt the Sea of Souls shift.

Reacting with speed and initiative Eldrad plunged into the Warp and looked for the source of the flames. A great psychic inferno to the galactic south lit up the Warp in a nonstop blaze of power that compared to the tempest of She Who Thirsts. Raw psychic power of a soul-scorching intensity and nature. Strengthening his wards Eldrad focused on the Fire-Storm and heard something. A faint song that played in the back of his mind. At first, he thought it was a simple whisper of the Warp but as he turned his genetically and psychically perfect mind to the song it started to make sense. It was not a song per say but psychic stimuli that would be interpreted as much by any psychic mind that could hear it.

After hours of refinement and psychic tuning, Eldrad managed to filter out all interference and felt the song in its full horror. It was horribly primitive and even when translated through his psychic senses and into Aeldari it was a coarse bawdy tune that was meant for any and all listeners not kin to the source.

Horror and disgust filled Eldrads souls and he needed to steady himself. Even without his precognitive abilities, he knew a Storm was coming, a storm that would shake the galaxy to its core.
