From a young age I have always enjoyed delving into information and absorbing as many facts and concepts as I could. Warhammer 40k is the most recent of these special interests and it has truly caught my attention. So I worked to learn everything possible about the franchise. After about a year or two of this, I decided to try my handwriting. My first attempts were nothing to write home about but I steadily improved. Crafting universes, saga's and concepts brings me joy and I use a system of "internal writing" and a few notes to keep it all straight. Basically, I mentally write out the story and take notes on what works and what does not. I am really enjoying writing this story and actually just got myself a Patreon (Jayfiction is creating Writing Stories | Patreon) to help support myself. Recording my creative process is actually something I am working on with the Patreon. I've got big plans for this universe and I hope the quality only improves from here.
