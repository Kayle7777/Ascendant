Ecclisarchial Prayer traditionally sang on The Feasts of the Primarchs and the Feast of the Emperor's Ascension


The Lesson the Lion Knight: we must always be ready to slay monsters and Daemons that threaten the Emperor and Imperium. For Loyalty is its own Reward.


The Warning of Fulgrim the Serpent: None but the Emperor can be perfect and seeking to surpass him and his works will lead to naught but ruin and depravity.


The Lesson of Jaghatai the Hawk: No matter what happens and no matter what trickery we shall remain loyal and ready to strike against the Alien the Mutant and the Traitor.


The Warning of Perturabo: The Rust Lord: The Emperor gifted mankind with emotions and reason, balance them to better serve your duty to Emperor and Imperium.


The Lesson of Russ the Wolf: Hunt down and destroy any and all enemies of Mankind no matter where they can be found, be they be Xeno from beyond the Stars or wayward siblings.


The Warning of the Night Haunter: We are Steeled against fear and protected by the Emperor's light and no monster born of darkness can withstand his Holiness and our Wrath.


The Lesson of Rogal the Praetorian: It's our duty to protect the Emperor and Imperium for, in turn, they will protect us and all we hold sacred. He on Terra endures eternally and so shall we.


The Warning of Angron the Butcher: The Emperor acts in mysterious ways and we all have a place in his plan and Imperium. To despise your role in that plan is heresy most foul, and will bring His Fury upon you.


The Lesson of The Iron Handed One: Be strong in the face of trials and tribulations. The enemies of the Imperium are many and we shall stand against them all no matter the cost, be it a sibling, a parent, or a child.


The Warning of Mortarion the Hypocrite: There is no way to use the tools of the enemy to slay the enemy, for the taint of the Alien, mutant and Heretic infects all they touch. It also tells us to respect the Emperor's Divine Authority and Judgment lest we stray from his Holy Light.


The Lesson of Lord Guilliman: Respect the Authority of the Emperor and his Imperium. Order and Faith form the Bulwark of the Imperium, and we each are tasked with maintaining this Bulwark with courage and honor.


The Warning of Magnus the Witch: Danger and damnation await users of Warpcraft and Sorcery. There are things not meant to be known by Mankind and those who seek them out doom themselves and all they love. Only through Faith and Devotion to the Emperor can true power and knowledge be obtained.


The Lesson of Vulkan the Eternal Flame: Through our works and devotions we can live eternal even after our body is dust and our soul goes to the Golden Throne. The truest legacy is not accolades or thrones but to help the Imperium with your life's works.


The Warning of Lorgar the First Heretic: No god but the Emperor exists and faith in false gods leads to ruin and damnation. Devotion and Duty are the greatest form of piety and never allow yours to waver.


The Lesson of Corax Raven-King: Justice comes to all, it is useless to delay it for the Judgment of The God-Emperor of Mankind will come to all who threaten his children. All those suffering beneath the Lash of the Alien, the taint of the Mutant or the Lies of the Heretic will be liberated and their oppressors shall face justice most ferocious.


The Warning of the Hydra: Lies and falsehoods will do nothing but postpone the inevitable, the Golden Light of the Emperor will burn away all trickery and deceit until the Truth of Mankind's Majesty and that of its Master are all that remain.


The Lesson of Sanguinius the Great Angel: All of us from the lowliest sinner to the Greatest of Angels can put our lives on the line to protect humanity and serve the Emperor. We are his Children and we are eternally indebted to him for his gift of eternal sacrifice and pain to protect us.


The Warning of Horus the Arch-Traitor: No matter how great the Hero, no matter how bright the Angel, all can fall and be corrupted. Be vigilant, be strong, for the Emperor is with us and none can stand against us!
